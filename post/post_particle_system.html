Simple Particle System

Abstract:

A simple structure of particle system using openGL based on C++


Details:

A basic particle system consists of two parts
  • Particle
  • Particle Generator
where the Particle has the attributes including position, velocity, color, size, etc. and the Generator is a controller of a swarm of particles. Another component of this system is called Generator Conllector, a higher encapsulation upon the generator. The Collector can control serveral same generators in different position
